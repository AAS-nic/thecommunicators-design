Dr. Rajesh Das

Dr. Rajesh Das is the faculty of the Department of Mass Communication, University of Burdwan, West Bengal, India since 2004. He did his PhD work in Health Communication from Visva-Bharati University, Santiniketan, India. He had completed a Major Research Project on Health Awareness in Rural Bengal through Television Coverage, funded by ICSSR -IMPRESS, Ministry of Education, Govt of India. As a collaborator he had also completed three research projects under IIMC, New Delhi , Ministry of Human Resource Development, Govt of India. Dr. Das has published 42 articles in an edited volume and/ or journal and presented 90 research papers in National/ International seminars/ conferences.

Prof (Dr) Mahul Brahma

Prof (Dr.) Mahul Brahma is an author, academician, a TEDx Speaker, an award-winning communication, branding and CSR leader, and a Chief Editor with two decades' experience in business communication and journalism. He is currently a Professor and Dean, School of Media and Communications at Adamas University, West Bengal. He is also a Visiting Research Fellow, School of Art, Film and Media of Bath Spa University, UK. Prior to Adamas University, Prof Brahma was Head of CSR, Corporate Communications, for a Tata group company ,'m-junction'. He has been a Chief Editor and has held other senior editorial positions in leading publications such as Economic Times, India partner of New York Times, among others. He is a luxury commentator, columnist and has authored six books. He is a PhD, D.Litt, MSc and PGP-Business Management, and an alumnus of IIM Calcutta, MICA, University of Cambridge. He has won many national and international awards, for his contribution, Sahityakosh Samman 2022, Corporate Communications Thought Leader of the Year 2022 are few of them.
