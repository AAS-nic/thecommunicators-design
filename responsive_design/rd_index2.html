About Us

The Communicators is a platform for all communication preceptors and professionals and is devoted to literacy, exploration, education, invention, creative exertion and public engagement. The Communicators is a forum to support scholars, scholars and communication professionals from various parts of the world and contribute to knowledge. The platform looks forward to integrating researchers, academicians, NGO and Corporate into a communication forum.
