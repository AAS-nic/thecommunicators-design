Association Membership

Organizational Individual
Eligibility: An application for Association membership must be accompanied by payment of specified fees for Association Membership. Once the petition for Association membership has been ratified by action of The Communicators Executive Board, the membership is immediately active. The highest ranking elected officer of the petitioning Association shall be designated as an Active Member of 'The Communicators'. Eligibility: Members of fully sanctioned Association Member organizations may become Additional Association Members of 'The Communicators' by having the Point of Contact submit their names and contact information to 'The Communicators' Member Services. Additional Association Members do not hold an ACTIVE membership, however, they may attend 'The Communicators events as members by paying the fees required of 'The Communicators' Active Members.
