About Us

The Communicators is a platform for all communication preceptors and professionals and is devoted to literacy, exploration, education, invention, creative exertion and public engagement. The Communicators is a forum to support scholars, scholars and communication professionals from various parts of the world and contribute to knowledge. The platform looks forward to integrating researchers, academicians, NGO and Corporate into a communication forum.

Become a Member Today!

Benefits of Becoming a Member of The Communicators: Membership comes with several benefits that have not been available to the communicators, especially in India, on a single platform. As any communicator would know, the foundation of professional development is being able to connect with an extensive network of peers at the multi-disciplinary level where members are drawn from a large array, including professionals working in the practice of communication and those engaged in the academic pursuits of communication.

The network includes members across the Globe, offering opportunities for international collaboration at various levels with opportunities to connect the practitioners with academics. It is this network of members that would also provide the space for creating mentor-mentee relationships that cross professional affiliations. In the end, membership of The Communicators would allow for a wide range of exposure to a significant resource of talent and expertise, all under the same umbrella.

The specific ways in which this would be accomplished would initially include

  • Regular Webinars (with a minimal fee under $5)
  • One academic conference every year
  • Opportunity to publish in a peer-edited book to be published annually
  • Opportunity to publish in a peer-edited academic journal at least once a year
  • Opportunity to publish in a quarterly e-journal which will include a listing of placement and academic opportunities
